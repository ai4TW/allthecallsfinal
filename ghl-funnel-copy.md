**Q: Does the AI actually sound like a real person?**
A: Yes. Our AI voice agents use the most advanced conversational AI available. Callers routinely don't realize they're speaking with AI. It handles pauses, interruptions, and complex questions naturally.

**Q: How long does it take to set up?**
A: Most businesses are live within 24 hours of signing up. You answer a short onboarding questionnaire, and we handle the rest — programming your AI, connecting your calendar, and configuring your call flow.

**Q: Can the AI book appointments to my calendar?**
A: Absolutely. Your AI receptionist connects to Google Calendar, Outlook, Calendly, and most major scheduling tools. It checks availability in real time and books confirmed appointments on the spot.

**Q: What happens if the AI can't answer a question?**
A: You set the rules. Your AI can transfer the call to you or a team member, take a message, or offer to schedule a callback. You're always in control of the escalation path.

**Q: Do I need to change my phone number?**
A: No. We set up intelligent call forwarding from your existing business number. Your customers call the same number they always have.

**Q: Is there a contract or commitment?**
A: No long-term contracts. All plans are month-to-month and include a 14-day free trial. Cancel anytime with no penalties.

**Q: What if I have multiple locations or phone lines?**
A: Our Pro and Agency plans support multiple lines and locations. Each line gets its own AI receptionist, custom-configured for that location's needs.

**Q: Can I customize what the AI says?**
A: Completely. You control the greeting, the tone, the FAQs, the booking flow, and the escalation rules. We work with you during onboarding to make the AI sound and act exactly the way you want.

- Nav links: Home | Pricing | Demo | Login

- Bottom row: Copyright 2026 AllTheCalls.ai | Social icons (LinkedIn, X/Twitter, Facebook)
- Background: #060710

### Typography Scale
- H1: 48-60px, bold, white
- H2: 36-44px, bold, white
- H3: 22-28px, semibold, white
- Body: 16-18px, regular, #a1a1aa
- Small/Subtext: 12-14px, regular, #71717a
- Section labels: 13-14px, uppercase, tracked wide, cyan or violet

### Button Styles
- **Primary:** Gradient fill (cyan-to-violet), white text, 16px, rounded-lg, padding 16px 32px, subtle box-shadow glow
- **Secondary/Outline:** Transparent fill, gradient border, white text, same sizing
- **Ghost:** No border, gradient text, underline on hover

### Card Styles
- Background: #111218
- Border: 1px solid #1e1f2a
- Border-radius: 12px
- Padding: 32px
- Hover: subtle border color shift to cyan or violet at 30% opacity

### Icon Style
- Line icons (not filled), 2px stroke
- Color: cyan (#4cd7f6) or gradient
- Size: 32-40px in feature cards, 24px inline
- Source recommendation: Lucide Icons or Phosphor Icons (both free, available as SVGs)
